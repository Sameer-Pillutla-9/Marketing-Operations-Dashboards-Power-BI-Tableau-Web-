# Dashboard Layout Notes

## Page 1 – Executive View
- KPI cards: Total Shipments, On-Time %, Avg Delay (hrs)
- Line chart: On-Time % over time (by hub or region)
- Bar chart: Late Shipments by region

## Page 2 – Hub Drilldown
- Slicers: Hub, Region, Date range
- Matrix: Date × Hub with Total Shipments, Late Shipments, On-Time %
- Column chart: Late Shipments by Hub

## Page 3 – Root Cause (optional)
- If you join delay reasons (weather, customs, facility), stack them by hub
  to show mix of causes over time.
